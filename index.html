The course

Welcome to the exciting world of mechatronics! The virtual course is designed to introduce kids to the fascinating field of engineering, combining mechanical, electrical, and computer science concepts to automate processes. Through interactive online projects and activities, students will learn how to design and build their own robots, exploring topics such as sensors, motors, programming, and mechanical systems. I'll guide your child through every step of the process, providing personalized feedback and support. By the end of the course, they will have developed a solid understanding of mechatronics principles and be able to apply that knowledge to solve real-world problems. Whether your child is interested in robotics, electronics, or programming, this virtual course is the perfect way to spark their creativity and curiosity. Join us from anywhere in the world and discover the endless possibilities of mechatronics!
